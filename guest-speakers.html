Guest Speakers

Charles Painter

Charles Painter is a project manager who is currently working at Epic on the popular title Fortnite.

Ben Burbank

Ben Burbank is a dialogue programmer currently at Valve and has had a key role on projects such as Firewatch and Half-Life Alyx.

Jesse Schell

Jesse Schell started his own studio Schell Games, published the book The Art of Game Design: A Book of Lenses, and has worked on titles such as I Expect You to Die and Until You Fall.

Christopher Floyd

Christopher Floyd has worked on titles such as Superliminal, Facebook Horizons, and Mini-Metro.

Alex Neuse and Mike Roush

Alex Neuse and Mike Roush are developers at Choice Provisions and worked on the popular title Bit Trip.
