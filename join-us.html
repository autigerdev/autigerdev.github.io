Yearly Dues

Want to make it official? To do so, every member of TigerDev is required to pay the high price of $10! These dues are on a yearly basis, and you can make them out to @Tiger-Dev on Venmo. By paying, you will also receive a fancy 'member' role on Discord and have the privilege of voting in semester elections.
