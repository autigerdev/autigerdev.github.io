Learn. Create. Play.



LEARN.

Compared to nearly any industry the video games industry is one of the youngest that exists. As such, many of the great discoveries in this field have yet to be explored. As game developers we have devoted ourselves to a life of continual learning. In order to engage in this philosophy TigerDev offers an array of opportunities to better yourself in whatever area of game development you are most interested in.


CREATE.

A passion for creating new and interesting experiences is at the core of TigerDev. Every semester we breaks out into teams where we conceptualize, prototype, and create brand new games from scratch. If you don't have time to devote yourself to a project then no worries because TigerDev also offers Game Jams several times throughout the semester!


PLAY.

In order to grow as developers and artists it's important that we recognize what the games before us did right as well as what they did wrong. In this pursuit, TigerDev chooses a "game of the month" for our members to get together and play. At the end of the month we come together and host a design discussion centered around every aspect of the game!
